0.2 PRELIMINARIES

Communication

Over the years, resizing files and having them maintain the same quality while functional can be worrisome for a data analyst, receptionist, student, or in any profession especially when it affects productivity.

Digitilization

Users will always settle for what they have or not delve into in completely when they have limited or no options. Users needed a platform to quickly upload their files and have a functional file in smaller bits to work with.

Bytsfy

CSV or JSON files can be resized into bits while maintaining the same quality as well as an option to download to computer and save for later.

DEVELOPMENT PHASE

Since it’s a thesis, the walkthrough shows progress of the website as no live development is in view at the moment. However, the team are working towards that in order to harness data around which parts of the product needed improvements, then ensure that data to focus on where to put the design effort.

Also, because peripheral experiences were underdeveloped and had little resources, we needed to find ways to efficiently stand them up and integrate them into our journey for launch.

DESIGN ITERATIONS

We recruited 5 participants from our slack community to conduct remote moderated usability testing on the designs. During the test, we asked them to complete a series of tasks that helped us to understand their thought process and see if they could accomplish the goal of the project. This was also the perfect time to ask about how they feel about the prototype. Below is feedback from the iteration. A primary button and secondary button was redesigned to help users differentiate the primary CTA which is the “save” button from the secondary CTA which is the “download” button.
